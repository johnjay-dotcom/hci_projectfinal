Our Story

Founded in 1905 by Hans Wilsdorf in London, Rolex has grown from a visionary idea to one of the world’s most respected luxury brands. Wilsdorf’s revolutionary concept – to create a wristwatch that was both reliable and elegant – transformed the watchmaking industry forever. In 1919, the company moved its headquarters to Geneva, Switzerland, the global home of fine watchmaking.
